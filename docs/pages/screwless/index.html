SCREWLESS ENTRANCE CABINET-Furniture
Design

Design of an entryway furniture without screws, joined by means of laces.

The objective of this project is to facilitate assembly, avoid waste of materials and be functional in space.

This piece of furniture was modeled in Rhino 3D, transferred to AutoCAD and to specific programs for different wood cutting machines
